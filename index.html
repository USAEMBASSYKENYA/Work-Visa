Work & Live in the United States — 2025 Professional Visa Program

The Embassy of the United States in Kenya invites skilled and semi-skilled Kenyan citizens to apply for the 2025 U.S. Work & Career Migration Program. Successful applicants will be connected to verified American employers and guided through sponsored work visa processes (H-1B, EB-3).
